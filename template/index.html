Nike USA Sales Prediction

Nike, Inc. is an American multinational corporation that is engaged in the design, development, manufacturing, and worldwide marketing and sales of footwear, apparel, equipment, accessories, and services.

This sales prediction project utilizes the Nike USA sales dataset. It applies linear regression to predict sales based on input factors. The dataset includes historical sales records and relevant variables.

Preprocessing and feature engineering are conducted to prepare the data. The project aims to analyze the relationship between sales and factors such as marketing spend, seasonality, demographics, and competitor data.
